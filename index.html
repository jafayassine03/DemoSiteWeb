About Us

Naomi Indonesian School (NIS) combines international educational standards with Indonesian values. Our passionate educators focus on holistic development — fostering curiosity, creativity, and character in every student. We offer both in-person and virtual learning to fit family needs.

Our Programs

STEM & Innovation

Hands-on projects, coding clubs, and maker-space activities that spark innovation.

Language & Math

Comprehensive language and mathematics tracks including HSK preparation, HSK exams, TOEFL preparation, Chinese language classes, and mathematics tutoring for all levels. Available online and on-campus.

Campus & Facilities

Our campus offers modern classrooms, digital learning labs, and creative spaces that inspire collaboration. We also provide a robust online learning platform for remote students.

  • Science & Computer Laboratories
  • Library and Reading Lounge
  • Virtual Classrooms & Online Learning Platform
  • Art & Music Studios (selected on-campus programs)
  • Cafeteria and Student Garden
